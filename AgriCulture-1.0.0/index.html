Smart Crop Advisory

KrishiSahay provides intelligent crop advisory support to farmers by analyzing seasonal conditions, soil type, and farming requirements. The system suggests suitable crops, fertilizers, and irrigation practices that help farmers improve productivity, reduce crop loss, and make better farming decisions with confidence.

Instant Agricultural Query Resolution

Farmers often face urgent problems related to pests, plant diseases, or crop health. KrishiSahay allows farmers to ask questions in simple language and receive instant, accurate answers. Using advanced knowledge retrieval and intelligent response generation, the system delivers clear guidance that can be easily understood and applied in real-life farming situations.

Multilingual Farmer Support

To ensure accessibility for all farmers, KrishiSahay supports multiple languages, including regional languages. This enables farmers to interact with the system in their preferred language, making agricultural knowledge more inclusive and easier to understand, especially for rural users with limited technical exposure.

Low-Connectivity and Offline Assistance

Many rural areas face poor internet connectivity. KrishiSahay is designed to work efficiently in low-network environments by providing cached and pre-loaded agricultural information. This ensures that farmers can access essential guidance even when internet availability is limited or unavailable.

Government Schemes and Market Awareness

KrishiSahay helps farmers stay informed about government schemes, subsidies, crop insurance, and market prices. By providing timely and relevant information, the system empowers farmers to take advantage of available opportunities, increase their income, and plan their farming activities more effectively.
